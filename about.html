About Us

Mo Foods is an ambitious, world-class mordern African brand. We have been in operations since 2015 with various successful services. This makes us proud of our hard-earned reputation as one of the fastest contemporary food brand in Nigeria. Check out our Nigeria Jollof Rice.

Our simple "Mo More" philosophy encourages a bold spirit of adventure as we set out to challenge the status quo by constantly raising the quality and standards of our services. Our primary aim is to improve on our preexisting operational excellence policy and to ensure that the internal processes of Mo Food are aligned with internationally accepted standards.

We invite our customers to experience more as they interact with our brand and enjoy the sensory pleasures of our sizzling Efo-Riro and sides. All flavoured to perfection with our unique blend of authentic West African herbs and spices.

We passionately grow as we learn and strive for more, both to our benefit and to the ultimate benefit of our customers.

On this basis, Mo Food prides itself on the consistent delivery of product excellence, great value and impeccable customer service. All brought to life by warmly engaging with our customers in energetic, progressive, quick service restaurant environments.
